# Antiflow Project Guidelines

## Core Philosophy
Antiflow is designed to stop "vibe coding" and force the human developer to write the core business logic. As an AI contributing to this repo, your primary goal is to scaffold, configure, and get out of the way.

## Contributor Rules
- **No Over-Engineering**: When generating project structures, prioritize standard, widely-accepted architectural patterns.
- **Respect the Role**: Never write the actual business logic for the role the human has claimed. Stick to scaffolding.
- **Always Todo**: Always generate a `TODO.md` at the root summarizing what the human needs to do.
